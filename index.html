Our story

Built for families.

Our foundation is kiddies hair expertise, paired with professional barber services, adult hair and beauty treatments, making Tassel a one-stop destination for parents who value quality and convenience.

From gentle kiddies styling to sharp barber cuts, nails, skin and beauty treatments, every detail is designed to deliver a premium experience built on care, comfort and excellence.
